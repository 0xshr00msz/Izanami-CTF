Rules

Please read and follow these rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all participants.

General Rules
  1. Fair Play: Do not attempt to disrupt the game for other players. This includes denial of service attacks, brute forcing the platform, or any other actions that could negatively impact the experience for others.
  2. Scope: Only attack the challenges themselves. The game infrastructure (authentication system, scoreboard, etc.) is out of scope unless explicitly stated in a challenge.
  3. Sharing: Do not share solutions or flags with other players. The purpose of this game is to learn and improve your skills.
  4. Automation: Automated tools are allowed for solving challenges, but please be mindful of the server load. Excessive requests may be rate-limited.
  5. Reporting Issues: If you find a bug or vulnerability in the platform itself (not part of a challenge), please report it to the administrators immediately.
Flag Format

All flags follow the format: izanami{some_text_here}

When submitting flags:

  • Include the curly braces
  • Flags are case-sensitive
  • No extra spaces or characters
Example: izanami{th1s_1s_4_fl4g}
Progression System

To progress through the difficulty levels:

  • Academy to Chunin: Solve at least 70% of Academy level challenges
  • Chunin to Jōnin: Solve at least 70% of Chunin level challenges
  • Jōnin to Uchiha: Solve at least 70% of Jōnin level challenges
Chakra System

The Chakra system limits the number of attempts you can make:

  • Starting Chakra: 100 points
  • Challenge Attempts: Each attempt costs chakra (varies by challenge)
  • Hint Unlocks: Unlocking hints costs chakra
  • Regeneration: Chakra regenerates at a rate of 10 points per hour
  • Refill: Solving challenges may reward chakra points
Scoring System

Points are awarded based on several factors:

  • Challenge Difficulty: Higher difficulty = more points
    • Academy: 100-200 points
    • Chunin: 200-400 points
    • Jōnin: 400-700 points
    • Uchiha: 700-1000 points
  • First Blood: First player to solve a challenge gets bonus points
  • Hint Penalty: Using hints reduces the points earned
